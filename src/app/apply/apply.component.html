How To Apply

We would like you to take a tour of the procedure to appy the DSC and get the delivery at your door step.

  • Step 1

    Make Selection

    Select the class(class2 or class3) and category(Individual/Organisation) according to your requirement.

  • Step 2

    Make Payment & Fill the form

    Click on button (Individual / Organisation) and make the payment using any available mode. After successful payment you will redirect to the application form.Fill the form carefully.

  • Step 3

    Complete Verification

    Once you fill your form, please verify your contact number and Email ID via OTP. Complete Video varification through the link or app as provided in your mail.

  • Step 4

    Upload Document*

    In case of organisation, Please upload the required documents and send it to register office.

  • Step 5

    Approval from registered Authority

    Within 1 hour, DSC Authority will send acceptance/Objection mail to your registed EmailID.In case of Objection, upload required documents.

  • DSC
    is
    Ready!

    DSC at your DoorStep

    We will send you the token. After receiving the token, reach out to us.
